AgriML: Smarter Crop Planning & Fertilizer Guidance

Why AgriML Matters for Farmers

AgriML is an AI-powered farming assistant that empowers farmers with
data-driven decisions for better productivity and sustainable agriculture.
Our platform tackles key farming challenges through smart predictions and recommendations.

AgriML reduces guesswork, maximizes crop output, and supports
sustainable resource usage helping farmers thrive in changing climates.

AgriML offers a suite of features designed to help farmers make informed decisions:

  • Choose the Right Crop: Get intelligent crop suggestions based on your soil, temperature, rainfall, and other conditions.
  • Boost Yield Accuracy: Predict crop yield before planting using environmental and historical data.
  • Smart Fertilizer Advice: Receive personalized fertilizer recommendations based on soil nutrients and crop type.
  • Step-by-Step Measurement Guide: Learn how to measure rainfall, soil pH, NPK, and other inputs for accurate predictions with our Interactive guide.
